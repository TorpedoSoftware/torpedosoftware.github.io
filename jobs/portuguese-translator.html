Portuguese Translator

A big part of Torpedo Software's founding philosophy is to make our content accessible to everyone, regardless of their native language. Translating the our projects is a important part of our mission, and you can be a part of it.

Role overview:

Type: Contractor Position, part time

Location: Remote

Duration: 1-2 months

Description: As the Portuguese Translator, you'll play a critical role in providing our empowering products to users in the Portuguese-speaking audience. You will work alongside our team(s) on several areas of our projects to offer our users a completely localized experience. You will oversee the language quality from end to end – from early localization to localization QA. Additionally, you will ensure that all content is appropriate for the Roblox platform and our young audience.

You are:

  • Fluent in English and Portuguese
  • Certified in localization or translation
  • Experienced with Roblox's translator dashboard and tooling
  • Passionate about making education accessible

    • You will:

  • Write high-quality translations for our product, marketing, and others
  • Identify & remedy linguistic and localization issues
  • Collaborate with our team to test and validate localization quality

    • You'll love:

  • Flexible work hours and vacation policy
  • Remote team around the world
  • Caring management that will always put your health first
  • Competitive wages and compensation
